Stamp Iterations

Thick, bold, billboard-readable. Impressed into surface. Six executions of one idea.
Iteration 01
Round Brand
Circular seal, debossed into dark plate. Anton — the heaviest readable weight. Hot-brand glow at the letter edges.
Iteration 02
Freight Stamp
Industrial rectangle. Triple border frame. Ya! at maximum scale — the word fills the container. Reads at any distance.
Iteration 03
Pure Impression
No container — the letters are pressed directly into the plate. Highlight top-left, shadow bottom-right. The surface is the logo.
Iteration 04
Ink on Paper
Traditional rubber stamp with ink bleed. SVG noise filter simulates uneven ink saturation at letter edges. Amber on aged stock.
Iteration 05
Iron Octagon
Eight-sided stamp mark — authority, precision, field equipment. Heavy border impressed into iron-dark surface. Vertex dots as registration marks.
Iteration 06
Amber Raised
Inverted: dark letters raised from an amber ground. The only version where the stamp reads as embossed rather than debossed. Ceremonial weight.
Type notes
All iterations use Anton — the heaviest readable weight available.
Black Ops One used in Iteration 04 for authentic stamp character.
Deboss/raise effects are SVG filter composites — no raster images.
